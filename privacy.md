# Vectra Privacy Policy

Vectra is a Google Sheets add-on that helps clean and standardize spreadsheet data.

## What we collect
- Your Google account email when you sign in to Vectra (Firebase Authentication).
- Spreadsheet data you choose to process in the active sheet.
- API keys you paste (e.g. Gemini) — used only to run cleaning you request.

## What we do not do
- We do not sell your data.
